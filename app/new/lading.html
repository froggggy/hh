What you can do

  • Easy & Quick Transformation

    Various documents used in your company can be easily and quickly converted to electronic format with the MS Office plug-in.

  • Electronic Signature

    E-Signature contains information such as pressure, signature information, location, time, and number of strokes, making all documents that require signing reliable.

  • Smart Mobile Form

    Supports various mobile environments such as iOS, Android and WinRT. It supports digital signatures and simple data input as well as various input functions such as photo, voice and barcode to create smarter electronic documents.

  • Secured Archiving

    All created documents are double-and-triple- distributed and stored securely in Amazon cloud storage and If you need it, use a time stamp to avoid forgery and modulation.
